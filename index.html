Space Mapper

An Android App for Studying Human Mobility and Activity-Spaces

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Space Mapper is a mobile phone app that collects information about the spaces we move through as we go about our daily activities. It is being used by researchers at Princeton University and the Center for Advanced Studies of Blanes (CEAB-CSIC) to study human mobility and social inequality.
